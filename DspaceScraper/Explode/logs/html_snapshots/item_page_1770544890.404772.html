Title: Behavioral Aspects of ERP Implementation: A Conceptual Review
Authors: Mitra, Prokreeti
Mishra, Sasmita
Keywords: ERP Implementation, critical success factors, perception of users, leadership
Issue Date: 17-Mar-2016
Publisher: IJIKM
Citation: Interdisciplinary Journal of Information, Knowledge, and Management Volume 11, 2016, pp. 17-30
Abstract: Recently ERP implementation has seen increasing significance in different sectors. The research related to the implementation issues of ERP has also increased during the past decade. Particular-ly the behavioral aspects of ERP implementations have been researched in terms of identifying appropriate frameworks, critical success factors, perception and attitude of users and managers, the role of change agents, leadership, etc. This conceptual review summarizes some of the studies done on the above aspects and suggests further research areas. It is suggested that leadership competencies, learning attitude of the users, and organizational power dynamics can be potential areas of research in the context of ERP implementation.
