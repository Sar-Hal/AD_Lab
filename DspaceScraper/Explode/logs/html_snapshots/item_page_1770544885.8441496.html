Title: EXPANSION STRATEGIES FOR LARGE VOLUME PARENTERALS (100ml infusions) IN THE CURRENT MARKET
Authors: Acharya, Subhranshu
Keywords: Promea Therapeutics
Issue Date: 25-Apr-2024
Publisher: KIIT UNIVERSITY
Abstract: Promea Therapeutics focuses on producing In Vitro Diagnostic (IVD) products, diagnostics, and medical equipment, with a particular emphasis on Large Volume Parenteral (LVP) products. This thesis aims to examine the introduction of 100ml LVP products into businesses across Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, and Karnataka regions, aiming to provide insights into potential clients and Promea Therapeutics' market presence. The main objectives include increasing commercial revenue and boosting Promea LVP sales in these regions, particularly in Hyderabad. As a Business Development Executive, my responsibilities involve developing a strategic marketing plan through extensive market research, client meetings, and analysis of sales data. I oversee internal market research initiatives to ensure seamless communication between field sales teams, export associates, and the corporate office. Crucially, I track field team sales activities to gauge overall performance, focusing on 100ml IV fluid sales and providing monthly sales reports for each zone's business development member. Conducting comprehensive market analysis was crucial to understanding the competitive landscape and our rivals' service areas. Market research enhances our understanding of target demographics and customer behaviors, enabling more effective marketing strategies. Leveraging data-driven insights aids in organizational growth and competitiveness, regardless of business size or type. Our efforts have resulted in a streamlined sales cycle, with targeted strategies and resource allocation leading to increased lead generation and conversion rates. Aligning our actions with a well-defined strategy has improved efficiency, efficacy, and ultimately, revenue generation
