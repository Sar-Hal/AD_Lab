Title: A Guide for Enterprises to Automate Financial processes
Authors: sahoo, Pragyan Paramita
Keywords: Leading supplier of financial technology
Issue Date: 24-Apr-2024
Publisher: KIIT UNIVERSITY
Abstract: Leading supplier of financial technology (FinTech) solutions, HighRadius assists businesses in automating and streamlining their treasury and accounts receivable procedures. Offering cutting-edge technological solutions that boost productivity, better cash flow management, and improve overall financial performance for companies in a variety of industries, HighRadius is a key player in the FinTech market. Their emphasis on industry leadership, innovation, and client success highlights their importance in influencing the direction of financial technology. The goal of HighRadius is to use cutting-edge technological solutions to completely transform the order-to-cash and treasury management procedures for companies. With the use of intelligent automation, machine learning, and AI-driven insights, they want to enable finance and accounting teams to increase productivity, save expenses, and enhance cash flow management. Key Products: a. Integrated Receivables Platform: HighRadius provides a full featured platform that automates a number of processes related to accounts receivable, such as cash application, credit and collections, administration of deductions, electronic payments, and customer correspondence. b. Cash Application Cloud: This technology automates the cash reconciliation and application process, saving manual labour and increasing accuracy via the use of AI and machine learning algorithms. c. Treasury Management: HighRadius offers treasury management solutions that maximize risk reduction, compliance, cash forecasting, and liquidity management for businesses.
